Our Story

Glow Studio was founded with the idea of creating a place where beauty is more than appearance — it’s harmony, relaxation, and confidence. Located in the heart of Tallinn, our salon combines professionalism with a warm atmosphere to make every visit special.

Our Philosophy

At Glow Studio, we believe in an individual approach to every client. Our values are:

  • Cozy and relaxing atmosphere
  • Experienced and caring masters
  • Use of high-quality products
  • Continuous training and growth
