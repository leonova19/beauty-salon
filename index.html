Our Salon

Glow Studio is a modern beauty salon located in the heart of Tallinn. We specialize in high-quality beauty treatments, offering our clients a warm and relaxing atmosphere. From manicures and facials to hair styling and massages, our team is here to make you feel beautiful, confident, and cared for.

Why Choose Us

At Glow Studio, we pay attention to every detail to make your visits truly special. Our team consists of experienced professionals who work with passion and expertise. We tailor treatments individually for each client, create a cozy atmosphere, and use only modern techniques and high-quality materials.
